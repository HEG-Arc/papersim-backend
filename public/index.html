Context

After years of training people to use Odoo with slides, videos or webinars, it’s time to let them play with Odoo!

Each year new features are improving Odoo, new employees are hired, digitization is expanding to all sectors and companies are faced with the need to (re)train their users. What if users were able to play with Odoo to learn to use new apps, to perform specific tasks or to discover the new functions? This is the promise of OdooSim, a simulation game designed to train users in a real life scenario where customers are placing orders and employees need to manage inventory, production and pricing by using the relevant Odoo modules.

OdooSIM is a serious game designed to immerse learners in game scenarios mimicking real world business models (distribution, manufacturing, logistic, ...). The simulator accelerate the time in order to condense the experience of the players.

Features

OdooSIM Features

OdooSIM’s main goal is to support on the job training of employees and will allow for custom business processes to be supported by the simulator. At present time, OdooSIM has the following features.

Module Based Automation

Automation of external actions

  • creation of SO from customers
  • reception and shipping of goods
  • payments of customer invoices
  • payments of supplier invoices

Automation of internal actions:

  • invoice from PO or SO
  • stock transferts

Acceleration of time

Trainee use the real system! No slides, videos or fake applications. But days can go by in minutes.

Unlimited Possibilities

Simulated Sales curves (pricing, marketing) for replayability.

Who are you?

OdooSIM is developed by the University of Applied Sciences Western Switzerland (HES-SO) in collaboration with Camptocamp SA.

Is this available to my country?

OdooSIM is developed as a SaaS, so there is no geographical restriction. However, currently the focus is on supporting French and English language. Let us know in the comment section of the registration form if you need other localisation.
